# Kinetic Gain Embedded

Board Decision Escalation Map is designed to open an embedded executive-intelligence engagement:

- convert handoff sprawl, reroute depth, and owner drift into explicit board-visible routing tradeoffs
- expose where named final ownership or added review coverage can recover decision speed before another lane is funded
- tie escalation coverage and board confidence to named operating surfaces instead of abstract process complaints
- convert scattered routing bottlenecks into one reusable intervention packet for boards and investors
